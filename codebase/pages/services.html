Project Structure
Codebase projects usually consist of 7-9 students: 6-8 Project Developers and 1 Project Manager who serves as the main point of contact between the team and the client. Project teams meet with clients at the project start to align project goals and scope, a mid-semester deliverable, and a final presentation to deliver the project. Each project typically runs for about 12 weeks, including a 2 week period at the end of the project after development ends for Q&A and Technical Handoff. Project teams communicate and meet with the client on a weekly basis to discuss project updates and progress.
