Frequently Asked Questions
What is CodeBase’s project structure?
CodeBase's project structure consists of 4-5 project teams, each with a project manager and 6-8 developers on the team. The project manager serves as the main point of contact between the team and the client.
What is the typical project timeline?
Although it may vary slightly depending on the project, each project typically spans about 12 weeks and includes a mid-semester deliverable at around Week 5 and a final deliverable at around Week 10. It also includes a 2 week period at the end of the project after development ends for Q&A and Technical Handoff.
