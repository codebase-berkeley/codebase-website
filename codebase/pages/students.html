What can you do at CodeBase?

  • Work with startups and companies on industry projects.

  • Design, develop, and consult on real world technical challenges, as well as build an impressive portfolio and resume.

  • Build professional relationships and network with fast-paced companies.

  • Be part of a close community of friends at Cal with many social opportunities.

  • Work in a fun, fast-paced environment with your fellow bears.

CodeBase is always looking for designers and developers to help on our projects. Right now we need people with experience, though we will start doing training next semester. If you are a designer familiar with Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, or InDesign or an experienced software developer apply below!
