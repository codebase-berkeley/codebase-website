About Us
We are a community of hackers, designers, and inventors at Cal, brought together by our love of building and solving problems through code. However, we also take pride in the diversity and wide range of non-technical talents within our CodeBase family. We love to make memories together – from semesterly retreats to boba runs and late-night study sessions. Our work is our passion, but that doesn’t mean we can’t have some fun along the way.
